# Agentic Frameworks - Research Notes

## Definition
Agentic Frameworks enable the development of autonomous AI systems capable of reasoning, planning, and executing tasks with minimal human intervention. They represent a shift from generative AI to systems that can take actions independently.

## Key Frameworks
- **CrewAI**: Multi-agent collaboration framework used in finance, risk management, and strategy development.
- **LangGraph**: Used for building stateful, multi-agent applications with cycles and memory.

## Core Characteristics
1. **Autonomy**: Minimal human intervention required
2. **Reasoning**: Systems can plan and adapt
3. **Multi-agent coordination**: Specialized agents collaborate on complex tasks
4. **Continuous validation**: Requires robust API management and model versioning

## Market Relevance
Agentic AI is transforming enterprise operations by enabling autonomous decision-making, anomaly detection, and strategic planning across domains.
